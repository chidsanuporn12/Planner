How to use this: Lifting runs Monday–Friday evenings as Push / Pull / Legs / Chest & Back / Arms & Shoulders, with cardio right after each session. Saturday and Sunday mornings keep the run + rope jump anchor instead, since there's no lifting those days. If a session feels heavy, keep the cardio after it easy — the point is the week holding together, not punishing yourself.

The weekly challenge up top is the one thing to chase for a clean win. Complete it, log your number, and watch the tally climb. Miss a day anywhere else and the plan still resets clean at 5am.
